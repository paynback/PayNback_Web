About PayNback

PayNback is India's first in-store shopping support app that offers rewards and discounts to consumers. Enjoy massive benefits and pre-reduced prices when you shop at PayNback-associated merchants. PayNback is not an eCommerce platform from which you can buy or sell stuffs, but a platform that connects you with your desired merchants, offering competitive prices, and significant cash back rewards and incentives for all parties involved, such as customers, referrers, merchants, and franchisees. Moreover, it helps you manage your purchase bill payments and budgeting.

Find your favourite stores, discover exclusive deals, and connect directly with them on PayNback through calls or chats. Need directions? We've got you covered. Enjoy seamless bill payments using UPI, your personal wallet, or by redeeming rewards.

PayNback is India's pioneering on-site, offline shopping companion designed to maximize your savings and rewards.

Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms, we connect you directly with your favourited local stores, offering exclusive deals, competitive prices, and substantial cashbacks.

Enjoy a world of benefits:

• Discover: Find nearby stores, explore their offerings, and uncover hidden gems.

• Save: Benefit from pre-reduced prices, exclusive discounts, and cashback rewards on every purchase.

• Engage: Chat directly with store owners, ask questions, and get personalized recommendations.

• Pay with ease: Settle bills effortlessly using UPI or redeem your PayNback rewards.

• Earn: Refer friends, onboard merchants, and earn attractive incentives, including cash rewards, commissions, and exciting prizes.

PayNback is more than just an app; it's your partner in smart shopping.

Vision & Mission

To be the world's leading platform for seamless and secure shopping experiences, empowering individuals and businesses everywhere with the freedom and flexibility of a rewarding and cashless future.

How does PayNback work?

PayNback is an online-offline e-commerce support platform connecting consumers with merchants. Our mobile app features a curated list of partner merchants offering unique deals and benefits. Customers can easily browse, select, and purchase products or services.

Another advantage is that customers can connect with desired merchants via telephone or chat. Settle your purchase bills instantly through the PayNback app by easily redeeming rewards, using your wallet, or paying via UPI. Enjoy rewards with every bill payment.

Enjoy exclusive perks as a PayNback customer, including special prices, discounts, and rewards.

We empower both consumers and merchants through our network. Customers earn rewards for purchases, while merchants gain access to a wider customer base.

PayNback is your one-stop shop for convenient and rewarding shopping.

For Customers:

• Discover Great Offers: Browse a curated list of PayNback merchants on our mobile app. Find stores with special prices, discounts, and unique offerings.

• Seamless Shopping: Easily search for your desired products or services, browse by category, or find specific stores.

• Exclusive Perks: Enjoy special offers and benefits available only to PayNback customers.

Joining as a Merchant:

• Simple Sign-Up: Get started by acquiring a referral link from our marketing team, sharing it on social media, or visiting our website www.paynback.in Download and Install PayNback mobile app from play store

• Open Referral link to sign up with basic information.

• Complete Your Profile: Provide essential information about your business, including licenses, tax details, location, bank account details, and at least four high-quality photos showcasing your shop and key products.

Once you complete the listing process, you can start offering your products and services to PayNback's vast customer base.
