FAQs

1. Are festival cashback offers better than regular offers?

Yes, festival cashback offers are usually better with higher percentages, lower minimum spends, and wider merchant coverage compared to regular offers.

2. Can I combine festival cashback with other discounts?

Usually yes, unless specified otherwise in the terms. Many platforms allow stacking cashback with existing discounts for maximum savings.

3. How long do festival cashback offers last?

Most festival cashback offers run for 1-4 weeks, with the best deals usually available 1-2 weeks before the main festival day.

4. Do I need to register for festival cashback offers?

Sometimes. Some offers require activation or registration before making transactions, so always check the offer details first.

5. What happens if I don't receive my festival cashback?

Contact customer support immediately with transaction details. Festival periods may have higher processing times, but delays beyond 72 hours should be reported.
