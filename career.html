Our hiring process

1. Application

You'll browse open positions (HR Manager, Accounts Manager, Sales Manager, Sales Executive) and apply through our application portal. We're looking for individuals with diverse backgrounds and a desire to learn.

2. Screening

If your application piques our interest, we'll reach out for a written/technical interview. The nature of this interview depends on the specific role.

3. Interview

This is where you'll meet the HR team and potentially other team members. It's a chance for us to assess your skills and experience, and for you to learn more about the role and company culture. Feel free to ask questions to understand the role and team dynamics better.

4. Discussion

After the interview, our HR team will have an internal discussion to evaluate your candidacy.

5. Decision

You'll be informed of our decision after this internal discussion.
