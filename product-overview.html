PayNback Product Overview

Discover PayNback's comprehensive product features, benefits, and how it works. India's first in-store shopping support app offering rewards, cashback, and exclusive discounts.

What is PayNback?

PayNback is India's first in-store shopping support app that offers rewards and discounts to consumers. Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms, PayNback connects you directly with local merchants, offering competitive prices, significant cashback rewards, and exclusive incentives.

Our platform benefits all parties involved - customers, referrers, merchants, and franchisees - while helping you manage purchase bill payments and budgeting effectively.

How PayNback Works

Simple steps to start earning rewards and enjoying exclusive benefits

Download & Register

Download the PayNback app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store and complete a quick registration process with your basic information.

Discover Merchants

Browse through our curated list of PayNback merchants. Search by category, location, or specific products to find the best deals near you.

Shop & Pay

Visit the merchant's store and make your purchase. Use PayNback for seamless payment processing with instant transaction confirmation.

Earn Rewards

Automatically earn cashback and reward points on every eligible purchase. Watch your rewards accumulate in your PayNback wallet.

Redeem & Repeat

Redeem your accumulated rewards for discounts on future purchases, gift cards, or cash withdrawals. Keep shopping and earning more!

Refer & Earn More

Invite friends and family to join PayNback. Earn additional rewards for every successful referral and help them discover great savings too.
